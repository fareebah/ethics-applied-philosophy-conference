Ethical Life in Unethical Times

Hybrid Graduate Conference · April 17–18

About

The Ethics and Applied Philosophy Graduate Conference is a student-organized event focused on urgent philosophical questions arising from contemporary crises. The conference emphasizes work from marginalized perspectives and aims to make philosophy responsive and engaged.

  • Moral Injury
  • Resilience
  • Repair
